About SPAM X

SPAM X Smart Emergency Response System is an intelligent web application developed to provide fast and reliable emergency assistance during critical situations.

Our platform helps users quickly access nearby hospitals, ambulance services, blood donors, and emergency contacts in real time using modern technology.

The goal of SPAM X is to reduce emergency response time and save lives through smart digital solutions.
